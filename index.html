The R.E.A.L. Framework

Four months. Four phases.
One honest journey from wounds to wisdom.

This isn't motivation. It's transformation. Each month, you move through one phase of the REAL Heroic Journey — live coaching, practical tools, guided reflection, and a community of peers doing the same brave work.

Month 1
R Reckoning

Name what's no longer working. Identify the roles, narratives, and expectations that have pulled you away from yourself. Strip away what was never yours to carry. This is where the truth begins.

Month 2
E Excavation

Meet what's been running the show beneath the surface — shame spirals, hidden beliefs, stories about failure or irrelevance. You'll uncover these patterns with compassion and reclaim the power they've been holding.

Month 3
A Awareness

Reconnect to what matters now. Claim your identity on your own terms. Rewrite the outdated narratives and strengthen your inner compass so your decisions come from alignment, not obligation.

Month 4
L Living Authentically

Step into leadership from the truth of who you are. Make aligned decisions, set boundaries that hold, and build a sustainable path forward. Renewed, relevant, REAL.

Questions you might have

Can I do this if I'm already in therapy? +

Absolutely. This work complements therapy beautifully. Therapy often explores the past; this program focuses on the present disconnect and the future you're building. Many participants are in therapy simultaneously and find the two deepen each other. If you're unsure, I'm happy to talk through it on a fit call.

What if I miss a session? +

Every session is recorded and available within 24 hours. While live attendance is ideal — the group dynamic is powerful — life happens over sixteen weeks. You won't fall behind. The integration prompts and workbook keep you connected to the work even when you can't be there live.

I'm a private person — do I have to share? +

You share only what you choose to. The group operates as a confidential brave space, not a pressure cooker. Some of the most powerful shifts happen through listening, not speaking. You'll find your own rhythm — and you'll be respected wherever that lands.

What kind of people are in this group? +

Accomplished professionals — leaders, executives, entrepreneurs, creatives — who are privately reckoning with questions that don't fit into their public identity. People who are done performing and ready to do the real work. If you're reading this page and nodding, you'll be in good company.

How much time should I expect weekly outside of sessions? +

Plan for 30–60 minutes per week for integration prompts and journaling. This isn't homework — it's the space where insights become lived experience. The more you put into the between-session work, the more the sessions themselves will give back.

What if I want deeper support after the group? +

Many people find the group opens something they want to explore more privately. After Wounds to Wisdom, you'll have the option to continue with private 1:1 coaching. But there's no pressure — this program stands powerfully on its own, and you'll leave with tools and awareness that continue working long after we close the container.

I did the Sprint — how is this different? +

The Sprint cracks something open. Wounds to Wisdom is where you walk through it. The Sprint introduces the REAL framework across four weeks; this program gives you a full month in each phase — with deeper tools, more practice, and sustained community. If the Sprint was the honest conversation, this is the honest transformation.
